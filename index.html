Our Mission

Iqra is the premiere academy for educational and character excellence. The mission of IQRA is to empower students with exceptional Islamic character and academics to thrive as honorable members in a global community.

IQRA feaures a challenging curriculum, dynamic social programs and engaging extra-curricular activies. Students from IQRA are prepared not only for the hereafter, but also as bright, educated, and qualified students looking to further their educations and launch their careers.
